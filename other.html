The Others

Sitting on computers isn't the only way I spend my time over the years. I have a few hobbies that I have on the side for when I want to pass the time.

YouTube Editing

As you may have been able to tell from my experience at the television studio, I enjoy the production of all forms of media and the processes and nuances associated with it. Apart from making my own YouTube videos in high-school, I have recently edited for a large female online personality with over 1.3 million followers across all of her platforms. I was able to produce some great content for her while I was working at home during the shutdown for a year, and learned alot from that experience.

Book Collecting

I love history and everything that has to do with it. That's why in 2018, when I found out how easily accessible 17th century books were, I began a collection of some wonderful pieces of art, and fragile history. I have been able to own some amazing pieces of history that rarely anyone else gets to read, touch, and hold. Whenever I buy a new antiquarian book, I revere it alongside all other books in my collection.

Video Game Collecting

I love to collect video games, and more recently I've begun collecting sealed video games, and enjoy finding good deals, and watching them appreciate in value over time. It's a part of my childhood, it's a part of other's childhood, and it's up to me as a collector and preserver to make sure these games stay good condition for future generations to enjoy as well.
